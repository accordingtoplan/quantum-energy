# Quantum Energy Square, Homepage Specification v1
Working document. Mobile-first. Companion to PDP Spec v1.1.
Reference logic locked from feedback: Rhode structure and calm, David repeated argument, Wildbran animation and structure, Painterland expressive type, Halfday yellow ownership, Graza personality, Magic Spoon structure. Story and action in under 30 seconds on mobile (per client brief).

Homepage is yellow's stage. Flavor takeovers live on the PDP.

---

## Block 0. Announcement Bar + Nav
Thin bar: free shipping threshold or subscribe offer, one line. Nav: Shop, Subscribe, Our Mission, Store Locator. Clean, Proper Wild style.

## Block 1. Hero
Full-width, yellow-owned. Expressive oversized headline as the graphic (Painterland move), product large and dimensional. Static image or micro-loop for v1 per client brief.
Headline territory: the outcome, not the product. Working direction: "Energy you can actually feel."
Dual CTA, David pattern: Shop + How it works (anchors to Block 3).
Rule: brand, category and promise understood in three seconds.

## Block 2. The Argument, First Statement
Type-only block, two lines: the two bad choices. Energy drinks spike and crash. Bars call sugar energy.
Then the turn: Quantum is the first energy bar built around how caffeine actually works.
Reference: David discipline, Rhode whitespace. Subtle scroll-in animation, Wildbran restraint.

## Block 3. The Energy Curve (shared signature module)
Same scroll-driven curve as PDP Block 3, slightly shorter version. The homepage earns its "How it works" CTA here.
The three numbers land at the end of the curve: 100mg. 10g. 0 crash.

## Block 4. Product Lineup
Flavor grid, each tile carrying its flavor color as a preview of the PDP takeover. Partially cut-off grid edge (David) so you sense there is more. Tap through to PDP.
Two sections eventually: Variety Packs + Single Flavors (per client brief), v1 shows one row plus variety pack tile.

## Block 5. Retail Spotlight
Walmart placement as credibility plus Store Locator CTA. Website as retail tool.
One line of copy, retailer logos, "Find us near you."

## Block 6. Moments Strip
Condensed version of PDP moment selector: horizontal scroll of three to five moments (2pm slump leading). Each links to PDP anchored at the moment tabs.
Sells the routine. Rhode-style lifestyle calm, real motion, no stock energy tropes.

## Block 7. Taste / Chef Block
Dessert-coded close-up plus the pastry chef line. Craveability moment before conversion blocks.
Reference: Bombon-level appetite appeal, Graza personality in one caption.

## Block 8. Social Proof
Mixed wall: review quotes, UGC clips, star summary. Halfday pattern of GIFs, testimonials and images blended. Quotes chosen for felt-effect language ("no crash").

## Block 9. Subscribe and Save
One clean module: savings, flexibility, single CTA. Klaviyo popup exists separately, this block is for scrollers.

## Block 10. The Close + Footer
Argument's final repetition, one line: "Ingredients you can't feel are everywhere. This is the one you can."
Footer with personality (Proper Wild reference), prominent email capture, wholesale and affiliate links per sitemap.

---

## Rules
1. Homepage repeats the argument twice (Blocks 2 and 10), the curve carries the proof once (Block 3).
2. Yellow dominant throughout, flavor colors appear only as previews in the lineup grid.
3. Animation budget: hero micro-loop, curve scroll-draw, gentle scroll-ins. Nothing else moves. Wildbran restraint, not Awwwards showing off.
4. Every block one thumb-length on mobile, story complete by Block 4, action possible from Block 1.

## Prototype scope
Fully built: Blocks 0-5, 10.
Static: Blocks 6-8.
Placeholder: Block 9.

1. Hero headline final wording (three options to be written before build).
2. Hero format v1: static vs micro-loop, depends on available assets.
3. Which flavor colors from the current Quantum site, pending screenshots for exact values.
